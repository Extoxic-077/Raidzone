Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve NexVault. This page explains what cookies we use and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit and preferences.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the platform to function. They enable authentication, shopping cart persistence, and security features. These cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences (like sidebar state) to improve your experience. You can clear these through your browser settings at any time.

Analytics & Advertising

We currently do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies. Any future additions to this list will be disclosed in an updated policy.

How to Control Cookies

All modern browsers let you view and delete cookies through their settings menus. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in or making purchases.
