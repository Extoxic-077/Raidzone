Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves instant, affordable access to premium digital content. From gamers topping up their Steam wallets to professionals renewing software licenses — NexVault makes it seamless.

Technology

NexVault is powered by a modern stack: Java 21 + Spring Boot 3 on the backend, deployed on hardened Linux servers with automated CI/CD pipelines. Our infrastructure is monitored 24/7 with sub-second alerting.

We take data protection seriously — all personal data is stored in India, encrypted at rest, and never sold to third parties.
