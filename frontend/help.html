Help Center

How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about orders, payments, and digital keys.

Orders & Delivery
How quickly do I receive my digital key? ▾
Your key is delivered instantly after payment confirmation — usually within seconds. Check your "My Keys" page or your email inbox.
Where do I find my purchased keys? ▾
Go to Account → My Keys. All purchased digital codes are listed there with copy buttons for easy redemption.
Can I cancel an order? ▾
Digital goods orders cannot be cancelled once the key has been revealed. If you haven't received a key, contact support immediately.
Payments
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept UPI, all major credit/debit cards, net banking via Razorpay, and international cards via Stripe. All payments are in INR.
My payment failed but money was deducted. What do I do? ▾
This typically resolves within 3–5 business days as a bank refund. If it hasn't resolved, email support@raidzonemarket.com with your transaction reference.
Is my payment information stored? ▾
No. We never store card numbers or CVV. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay and Stripe — both PCI-DSS compliant.
Keys & Redemption
My key doesn't work. What should I do? ▾
First verify the key is entered without spaces. Check region compatibility (some keys are India-only). If the key is invalid, contact support within 24 hours with proof.
How do I redeem a Steam key? ▾
Open Steam → Games → Activate a Product on Steam → enter your key. It takes under a minute and the game appears in your library immediately.
The key was already used. What happens? ▾
This should never happen — all keys go through a validity check before listing. Contact support immediately and we'll replace it or issue a full refund.
Account
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▾
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send a 6-digit reset code.
Can I change my email address? ▾
Yes. Go to Profile → Change Email. You'll need to verify the new address with an OTP code sent to it.
