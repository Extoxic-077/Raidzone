DMCA Policy

Last updated: January 2025

RAIDZONE respects the intellectual property rights of others. We respond promptly to valid copyright infringement notices submitted in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe content on RAIDZONE infringes your copyright, submit a notice to our designated DMCA agent with the following information:

  • Your full name, address, phone number, and email
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim is infringed
  • The URL or specific location on RAIDZONE where the infringing content appears
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorised by the copyright owner
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on their behalf
  • Your electronic or physical signature
DMCA Agent:
RAIDZONE Legal Team
Email: dmca@raidzonemarket.com
Response time: within 48 hours on business days

Counter-Notices

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice containing: your contact details, identification of the removed content, a statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake or misidentification, and your consent to jurisdiction.

Repeat Infringers

In appropriate circumstances, RAIDZONE will terminate accounts of users who are repeat infringers of intellectual property rights.

False Claims

Submitting a false DMCA notice may result in liability for damages. Only submit notices for content you genuinely believe infringes your copyright.
